Our Story · ჩვენი ისტორია

Rooted in the hills of Guria,
brewed on the Black Sea coast.

Georgia gave the world wine. We wanted to return the favour — with coffee worthy of the same 8,000-year hospitality.

Georgian Coffee Drive was born on the rainy mornings of Batumi, when the sea fog rolls into the boulevard and every corner café is already full. We asked a simple question: what if you never had to leave the car to drink extraordinary coffee?

We source single-origin beans from Ethiopia and Colombia, then finish each drink with Georgian character — wildflower honey from Samegrelo, rose petals from Kakheti, walnuts from Racha, and the sweet string of churchkhela folded into our signature mocha.
